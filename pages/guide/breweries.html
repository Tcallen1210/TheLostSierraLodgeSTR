Breweries & Bars

From forest-hidden pints to mountain-town taverns — here are the go-to spots for a beer, a cocktail, and a good time out. Each listing includes quick links for info and one-click directions from the lodge.


The Brewing Lair

10234 Highway 89, Graeagle, CA

Their seasonal hours are:
April: Friday – Sunday, 12 PM – 5ish PM
May – October: Everyday, 12 PM – 8ish PM

The Brewing Lair in Graeagle is a fantastic spot for craft beer enthusiasts! This local brewery offers a wide variety of delicious beers brewed on-site, from hoppy IPAs to smooth stouts. Just 7 minutes from the Lost Sierra Lodge, The Brewing Lair is our favorite dog-friendly brewery with all outdoor seating, a food truck, and live music starting in late May. You can even bring your own food to enjoy!

Ronin Fermentation Project

601 Graeagle Johnsville Rd, Graeagle, CA 96103

Winter Hours:
Monday thru Friday: Closed
Saturday: 12–7 PM
Sunday: 12–5 PM

The Ronin Fermentation Project in Graeagle is a unique local spot only 10 minutes from the Lost Sierra Lodge where you can experience craft beverages made from fermented ingredients. They offer a variety of fermented products like kombucha, ciders, and other creative brews, all crafted with quality and care. It’s a great place to stop by for tastings, to enjoy a drink in their taproom or on the patio, or to pick up some local, artisan growlers or tall cans to go! (Check out their Facebook page for the karaoke and events schedule!)

Eureka Peak Brewing Co.

72056 CA-70, Portola, CA 96122

Right in Portola at Chalet View Lodge: a convenient taproom stop when you want something close. Grab a pint, relax, and keep the night easy — perfect for a quick “one more” without a long drive.

The Peak

7482 CA-89, Graeagle, CA 96103

A Graeagle favorite for a relaxed nightcap: wine, craft beer, and cigars in a mellow, grown-up setting. Great choice when you want a slower vibe after dinner or live music in town.

Website Directions

Knotty Pine Tavern

7426 CA-89, Graeagle, CA 96103

A classic, no-frills mountain tavern—casual, friendly, and exactly what you want when you’re coming off the lake or a long day outdoors. Great for a simple drink and an authentic local feel.

Info Directions

Mohawk Tavern

999 Johnsville Rd, Graeagle, CA 96103

A laid-back local stop near Graeagle—solid choice when you want a straightforward, small-town tavern feel. Think simple drinks, friendly faces, and an easy place to land after a day in the Lakes Basin.
